Project Goal

Can AI help evaluate Chipotle as an investment?

This project uses ChatGPT as a finance assistant, not as a replacement for judgment. The goal is to explore how AI can organize data, perform calculations, explain FIN 3010 concepts, and reveal where assumptions or hallucinations can weaken an analysis.

Research question: Can ChatGPT help us decide whether Chipotle Mexican Grill looks financially attractive by applying FIN 3010 concepts from Chapters 1–3, 5–6–9, and 7–8–12–13?

DuPont Identity

How we got the data

We opened Chipotle Mexican Grill's 2025 Form 10-K and used the consolidated balance sheet and income statement. Revenue, food and beverage revenue, operating expenses, and net income come from the income statement. Current assets, total assets, liabilities, and shareholders' equity come from the balance sheet. The figures are kept in thousands because the ratios are unaffected when the same unit is used consistently.

Critical Evaluation

Where AI was helpful

  • Organized large amounts of financial data into a clean framework.
  • Explained formulas like ROE, DuPont, DCF, and CAPM in plain language.
  • Made it easy to test multiple growth and discount-rate assumptions.
  • Helped turn calculations into a readable report structure.
  • We used ChatGPT to help generate the initial HTML structure for this GitHub page. We reviewed, edited, and customized the page to match our FIN 3010 project, Chipotle data, course concepts, calculations, prompt log, and reflection.
Judgment

Where AI needed checking

  • Financial data had to be verified from official filings.
  • DCF output depended heavily on assumptions.
  • High ROE needed context because repurchases and equity changes can affect it.
  • CAPM inputs are estimates, not facts.
